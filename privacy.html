Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Last Updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how VanQuick Tech ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information related to The Cascade Hangover League SMS notification service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information from league members:

  • Phone numbers — provided voluntarily by members for SMS communication
  • Names — for league identification purposes
  • Consent records — date and method of consent (verbal)

How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number exclusively to send SMS messages related to The Cascade Hangover League activities, including but not limited to:

  • Poll reminders and voting deadlines
  • Draft announcements and scheduling
  • League updates and rule changes
  • Weekly recaps and power rankings
  • Trade and waiver notifications

Information Sharing — We Do Not Share Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your phone number, opt-in data, or any personal information with third parties.

Your information is used solely for The Cascade Hangover League SMS communications and is not shared with any external marketing services, advertisers, or other organizations.

The only exception is our SMS delivery provider (Twilio), which processes messages on our behalf and is bound by their own privacy obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Your phone number and consent records are stored securely and access is limited to the league commissioner. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any SMS message to stop receiving messages at any time.
  • Help: Reply HELP to any SMS message for assistance and contact information.
  • Data deletion: Contact vanquicktech@gmail.com to request deletion of your phone number and consent records.

Data Retention

We retain your phone number and consent records for as long as you are an active member of the league. Upon opting out or leaving the league, your information will be deleted within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
