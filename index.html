The Constitution

League Rules

The law of the land. Know it. Live it. Exploit it.

Format

Dynasty league, 10 teams, hosted on the Sleeper platform. Built to last.

Roster

1 QB · 2 RB · 2 WR · 1 TE · 2 FLEX · 1 DEF
25-man roster + 5 taxi squad spots

Scoring

Full PPR — 1 point per reception. Reward the pass-catchers.

Waivers

FAAB system with a $100 budget. Continuous waivers — no waiting.

Trades

Open year-round. No trade deadlines in dynasty. Make your moves.

Draft

One draft — all players on the board, veterans and rookies alike. Date TBD by vote.

Playoffs

Weeks 14–16. Top 6 teams qualify. Seeds 1 & 2 earn a first-round bye.

Taxi Squad

5 spots reserved for rookies only. Stash your future without burning a roster spot.

Buy-In

To be determined. Open vote pending — see below.
