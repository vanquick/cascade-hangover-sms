League Rules

The official bylaws of the CHL

Dynasty league — your roster carries over year to year. 10 teams competing on the Sleeper platform. Build for now and the future.
Starters: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 2 FLEX, 1 DEF
Bench: 25-man total roster
Taxi Squad: 5 spots (rookies only, can be called up anytime)
PPR — 1 point per reception. Standard scoring otherwise. Every catch counts, so load up on pass-catchers.
FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget) — $100 budget per season. Continuous waivers. Spend wisely — when it's gone, it's gone.
Trades are open year-round. No trade deadline in dynasty — wheeling and dealing never stops. Trade players, draft picks, and FAAB.
Startup Draft: All NFL players available. Build your dynasty from the ground up.
Annual Rookie Draft: 5 rounds each year for incoming rookies.
Weeks 14–16. Top 6 teams make the playoffs. Top 2 seeds earn a first-round bye. Single elimination. Win or go home.
