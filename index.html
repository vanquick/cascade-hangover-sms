The Constitution

League Rules

The law of the land. Know it. Live it. Exploit it.

Format

Dynasty, 10 teams, Superflex · Half PPR · TE Premium. Hosted on Sleeper. Built to last.

Roster

1 QB · 2 RB · 3 WR · 1 TE · 2 FLEX · 1 SUPER FLEX
10 starters · 16 bench · 4 taxi · 3 IR — 33 total roster spots

Scoring

Half PPR (0.5 per reception) + TE Premium (extra 0.375 per TE catch). Passing TDs: 4. Rushing/Receiving TDs: 6.

Waivers

FAAB system with a $100 budget. Continuous waivers — no waiting.

Trades

Trade deadline: end of Week 12. Vetoes require 5 league votes.

Startup Draft

30-round snake on Saturday, August 1 at 3:00 PM PDT. Rounds 1–5 use a 2-minute pick clock. Rounds 6–30 shift to a 4-hour clock with an overnight pause from 11 PM – 8 AM PDT (picks can still be submitted during the pause).

Playoffs

Weeks 15–17. Top 6 teams qualify. Seeds 1 & 2 earn a first-round bye.

Taxi Squad

4 spots for rookies and second-year players (vets allowed). Stash your future without burning a roster spot.

Buy-In

To be determined. Open vote pending — see below.
