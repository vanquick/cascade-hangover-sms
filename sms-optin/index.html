SMS Notification Consent

How Consent Is Collected

Consent for SMS notifications is collected verbally by the league commissioner (Josh Van Quick) when each member joins the league. The commissioner confirms the member's phone number and explains that they will receive league-related SMS messages. Members voluntarily provide their phone numbers for league communication purposes.

Verbal consent is recorded and stored by the league commissioner along with the date of consent and the member's phone number.

By joining The Cascade Hangover League on Sleeper, members agree to receive SMS notifications related to league activities including:

  • Poll reminders and voting deadlines
  • Draft announcements and scheduling
  • League updates and rule changes
  • Weekly recaps and power rankings
  • Trade and waiver notifications

Details

  • Frequency: Estimated 5-15 messages per month
  • Message & data rates may apply
  • Contact: vanquicktech@gmail.com

SMS Keywords

You can use the following keywords at any time by replying to any message from our number:

  • STOP — Reply STOP to opt out and stop receiving all SMS messages from The Cascade Hangover League.
  • HELP — Reply HELP for assistance. You will receive a message with contact information and instructions.

After sending STOP, you will receive a one-time confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe by contacting the league commissioner.
